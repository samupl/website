As a part of my job at MyDevil.net I've created a front-end interface for the management system (in which creation I have also participated).

The front-end, called DevilWEB2 is created in Python (Django Framework), using Bootstrap 3 and jQuery. It allows any customer to easily manage his hosting account, including the following aspects:

  • Websites and DNS zones,
  • Mail accounts,
  • Databases (MySQL, MongoDB and PostgreSQL),
  • FTP accounts,

... and many other features.

At the time of creating this page, DevilWEB2 is widely used across all servers and services provided by MyDevil.net. It has proved itself to be fast, secure and has been many times acknowledged as the best, easiest to use web-based management system.
